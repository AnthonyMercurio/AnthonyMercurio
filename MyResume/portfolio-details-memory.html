My Sister's Battle Against Cancer

"Memory" dives into my childhood, seen through the lens of my parents' experiences. At the age of 3, my younger sister Malena was diagnosed with Leukemia and Spinal Meningitis, two intimidating illnesses that put an abrupt halt to her carefree childhood. However, after a long 3 year battle, she triumphed over both conditions by the age of 6. The film sheds light on the journey my parents, siblings, and I experienced during that challenging time. It also acknowledges the critical role played by individuals like Lisa Booz and organizations such as Camp Good Days, all of which provided vital support in helping us reclaim the happy childhood that cancer had stolen from us. Utilizing found footage and images, and videos and audio recordings, I sought to authentically capture our experiences. Through this project, I gained valuable experience acting and conducting cinematography.
